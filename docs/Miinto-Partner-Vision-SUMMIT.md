# 2. The Problem

## Onboarding Is the Bottleneck

Every week, boutiques across Europe want to join Miinto. But joining takes 2-4 weeks of manual work:

- ✉️ Email back-and-forth to collect documents
- 📄 Print, sign, scan contracts
- 📞 Multiple calls with our team
- ⏳ Days of waiting between each step

**Result:** Great boutiques give up. Our team is the constraint. We lose partners to competitors.

**Current State:**
| Metric | Today |
|--------|-------|
| Avg onboarding time | 2-4 weeks |
| Manual touchpoints per partner | 15+ |
| Team capacity | ~20 partners/month |
| Partner waitlist | Growing |

# 4. The Vision

## 18 Months. 10x Partner Growth. One Modern Platform.

**By December 2027:**
- Partners grow from 600 → 6,000
- Onboarding time: 2-4 weeks → **48 hours to onboard**
- **Time to first order: < 72 hours** ← *true north star metric*
- Team capacity: No longer the bottleneck

**The Companion App** is the single platform where every partner:
- Discovers Miinto and applies
- Signs contracts digitally (legally binding, EU-compliant)
- Completes training at their own pace
- Sets up their integration in minutes
- Manages orders and operations
- Gets support self-service

**Our vision:** Make every boutique in Europe a Miinto partner.

# 5. The Companion App — 6 Capabilities

| Capability | What Partners Do |
|------------|------------------|

| **Sign** | Review and sign contracts digitally — no printing, no scanning |

| **Sell** | Manage orders, inventory, returns, performance from one dashboard |
| **Get Help** | Find answers instantly in the knowledge base, no phone calls needed |
| **Configure** | Set up API keys, webhooks, integrations in minutes |

# 6. The Partner Experience — 48 Hours to Onboard, 72 Hours to First Order

> **⚠️ Caveat on signing SLA:** The 48h target assumes partner responsiveness. DocuSign signing has no enforceable SLA — partners can delay up to 14 days. Mitigations: automated reminder escalation (Hours 2, 4, 8, 24) and signing deadline clause in new partner contracts. See §10 Risk #3.

```
HOUR 1                    HOUR 8                   DAY 2                   DAY 3+
│                        │                        │                       │
▼                        ▼                        ▼                       ▼
Discover & Apply      Verify & Sign           Connect & Test         LIVE & SELLING
- Visit website       - Upload docs           - Connect Shopify       - Orders flow
- Fill application    - Auto KYC verify      - Test in sandbox      - Ship orders
- VAT auto-verify     - Sign digitally       - Fix any issues       - Monitor dashboard
- Account created      - Contract legally     - Go live checklist    - Support if needed
```

**Hour 1:** Boutique in Copenhagen hears about Miinto. Visits website, clicks Apply, fills company details. VAT verified automatically. Account created within the hour.

**Hour 8:** She uploads documents. KYC runs automatically. Reads and signs contract on her phone. No printing, no scanning. *(If partner is unresponsive, see signing escalation above)*

**Day 2:** Connects her Shopify store using our pre-built integration. Runs a test in sandbox. Fixes one config issue. Goes live.

**Day 3+ (TARGET):** First order received within 72 hours of starting application. She ships. Miinto handles the rest.

# 9. NTS Migration Synergy

**The Companion App and NTS migration are ONE project, not two.** We use the NTS migration as the forcing function to modernize our entire partner base.

> **⚠️ RECOMMENDATION:** Decouple at ship time — new partners from Q2 2026 enter on POAS v2.5 stack. NTS migration continues in background. Legacy partners migrate with full support. This prevents NTS delays from blocking Companion App delivery.

| Partner Type | Timeline | Experience |
|--------------|----------|------------|
| Already on NTS | Early access Q3 2026 | Full Companion App |
| New partners | From Q2 2026 | Modern system from day 1 (POAS v2.5 + Companion App) |
| Legacy partners | Migrate by Dec 2026 | Companion App + migration support |

**NTS Readiness Gates:** Each Companion App phase requires NTS dual-run divergence <0.05% for 30 consecutive days. NTS schema frozen 30 days before each release.

**By end of 2026:** One modern system — not a split between old and new.

---

# 10. Key Risks & Mitigations

|| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| **NTS coupling** (NEW) | 🔴 Critical | Companion App ships on POAS v2.5 for new partners; NTS migrates in background. NTS schema frozen 30 days before each Companion App release. |
| **DocuSign partner non-responsiveness** (NEW) | 🔴 High | Automated reminder escalation at Hours 2, 4, 8, 24. Signing deadline clause in new partner contracts. "Signing completion rate" as operational KPI. |
| Integration complexity | High | Phased rollout. White-glove support for high-value partners with legacy systems |
| Market variation (regulatory) | Medium | Partner Advisory Board to surface market-specific issues early |
| Adoption resistance | Medium | Named success manager for first 100 partners. Gather feedback, iterate |

# Competitor Analysis — Fashion E-commerce Platforms

## Overview

| Platform | Type | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|----------|------|-----------|------------|
| **Zalando** | Pure e-commerce + Marketplace | Leader EU, brand forte, logistica eccellente | Partner onboarding complesso,supporto B2B carente |
| **Farfetch** | Marketplace luxury | Brand prestige, unboxing experience | Onboarding manuale, solo luxury |
| **Vinted** | C2C + B2C | No fees for sellers, user base large | Supporto scarso, policy inconsistenti |
| **About You** | E-commerce + Marketplace | UX personalizzata, dati cliente | Dependence on Zalando post-acquisition |
| **ASOS** | Pure e-commerce | Gen Z focus, Own-brand forte | Non marketplace model, limited B2B |

---

## Zalando — Direct Competitor

### Company Profile
- **Headquarters**: Berlin, Germany
- **Presence**: 25+ countries
- **Revenue**: €10B+ (2023)
- **Employees**: 17,000+

### Partner Program

#### Strengths
1. **Scale** - Largest fashion e-commerce in Europe
2. **Logistics** - ZEOS fulfillment option
3. **Brand** - Alto awareness
4. **API** - zDirect API mature
5. **Support** - Partner portal dedicato

#### Weaknesses
1. **Complex Onboarding** - Multi-step, document-heavy
2. **Long Time-to-Market** - Weeks to go live
3. **High Fees** - Commissioni elevate (15-40%)
4. **Support Quality** - Partner complaints on Trustpilot
5. **Rigid Rules** - Compliance burden for small boutiques

### Partner Portal Features
- Dashboard ordini real-time
- Analytics vendite
- Product catalog management
- Returns dashboard
- Support ticketing

### Reviews (Trustpilot)
- **Rating**: ~2.5/5 per partner portal
- **Common Complaints**:
  - Slow support response
  - Complex fee structure
  - Payment delays
  - Strict policies

### Connection to Miinto
Zalando is Miinto's biggest competitor in the Scandinavian/Nordic region where Miinto is strongest. Miinto differentiates by focusing on local boutique curation vs Zalando's mass-market approach.

## Farfetch

### Company Profile
- **Headquarters**: London, UK
- **Presence**: 190+ countries
- **Revenue**: €2.3B (2023)
- **Model**: Marketplace luxury

### Partner Program

#### Strengths
1. **Brand Prestige** - Luxury positioning
2. **Global Reach** - International customers
3. **Express Delivery** - BRBO service
4. **Quality Control** - High bar for partners

#### Weaknesses
1. **Manual Onboarding** - Contact-based, no self-service
2. **Elite Only** - Not accessible to small retailers
3. **Slow Process** - Approval can take months
4. **High Commission** - 15-30% typical

### Onboarding Process
1. Application submission
2. Brand vetting
3. Interview/call
4. Contract negotiation
5. Technical integration
6. Soft launch
7. Full launch

### Reviews
- Positive: Brand exposure, premium customers
- Negative: Slow, bureaucratic, exclusivity requirements

---

## Vinted

### Company Profile
- **Headquarters**: Vilnius, Lithuania
- **Presence**: EU + US
- **Model**: C2C fashion marketplace
- **Users**: 80M+ members

### Partner Program
Vinted does NOT have a traditional B2B partner program - it's C2C focused.

#### Strengths
1. **No Seller Fees** - Free to list
2. **Massive User Base** - 80M+ members
3. **Mobile-First** - Great app experience
4. **Sustainability Focus** - Eco-friendly positioning

#### Weaknesses
1. **No B2B Model** - Not applicable for Miinto's use case
2. **Poor Support** - AI bots, slow human response
3. **Policy Issues** - Seller protection inadequate
4. **Counterfeit Problems** - Limited verification

### Relevance to Miinto
Limited - Vinted is C2C while Miinto is B2B fashion aggregation.

## About You

### Company Profile
- **Headquarters**: Hamburg, Germany
- **Presence**: DACH + international
- **Revenue**: €1.5B+
- **Status**: Acquired by Zalando (2024) for €1.2B

### Partner Program

#### Strengths
1. **Personalization** - AI-driven recommendations
2. **Youth Focus** - Gen Z appeal
3. **Fashion Forward** - Trend-forward curation
4. **Data Access** - Customer insights for partners

#### Weaknesses
1. **Acquisition Impact** - Integration with Zalando may change terms
2. **Regional** - Strong in DACH, weak elsewhere
3. **Smaller Scale** - Limited compared to Zalando

### Onboarding
- Online application
- Review process
- Contract agreement
- Technical setup

## ASOS

### Company Profile
- **Headquarters**: London, UK
- **Presence**: Global
- **Revenue**: £3.3B
- **Model**: Pure e-commerce (not marketplace)

### Relevance to Miinto
ASOS is NOT a direct competitor for partner onboarding because:
- They don't operate a marketplace model for third-party sellers
- They retail own-brand and branded inventory
- No B2B partner program

## Key Takeaways for Miinto

### Opportunities (Punti di Forza da Emulare)
1. **Zalando's Portal** - Mature partner dashboard
2. **About You's UX** - Clean, modern interface
3. **Farfetch's Quality** - High partner standards

### Threats to Address (Debolezze da Evitare)
1. **Manual processes** - Main friction point everywhere
2. **Slow support** - Kills partner satisfaction
3. **Complex docs** - Partners want simple

### Differentiators for Miinto
1. **Local Boutique Focus** - Curated, not mass-market
2. **Scandinavian Efficiency** - Fast, modern, trustworthy
3. **NTS Migration** - Modern tech stack opportunity
